PRIVACY POLICY
SANSKRIT.EXE is built on a simple principle: your learning data belongs to you, on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.
SECTION 01
DATA WE COLLECT
We collect nothing. SANSKRIT.EXE has no servers, no accounts, no sign-in, and no data transmission. Every piece of information about your progress exists only in your browser's localStorage — on your device, under your control.

The following data is stored locally in your browser using the Web Storage API (localStorage):

LOCAL STORAGE KEYS
  • Lesson progress and completed modules
  • Daily streak count and last-active date
  • Achievement badges earned
  • Daily quest completions
  • Answer history and accuracy statistics
  • UI preferences (theme, sound settings)

This data never leaves your browser. It is not accessible to us or to any third party. Clearing your browser's localStorage will permanently erase it.

SECTION 02
ANALYTICS & TRACKING

SANSKRIT.EXE uses no analytics software, no tracking pixels, no fingerprinting, and no advertising networks. We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Hotjar, or any equivalent service. There are no cookies set by this application.

SECTION 03
THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The only third-party service used is Google Fonts, which serves the typefaces (VT323, Share Tech Mono, Noto Sans Devanagari) used across the interface. When your browser loads the page, it requests these font files from Google's CDN servers. Google may log this request, which includes your IP address, in accordance with their own privacy policy.

No other external resources, CDNs, or APIs are contacted by this application.

SECTION 04
YOUR RIGHTS & CONTROL

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

WHAT YOU CAN DO
  • View your data in your browser's DevTools under Application → Local Storage
  • Delete all data by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site
  • Use the app in private/incognito mode — no data persists after the session

There is no account to delete, no server-side record to request, and no data erasure process to initiate — because we never had your data to begin with.

SECTION 05
CONTACT

Questions about this policy? Reach us at contact@sanskrit.exe. We will respond as soon as we can.
